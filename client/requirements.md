## Packages
framer-motion | Essential for complex scroll-driven animations and layout transitions
howler | Robust audio handling for the background music

## Notes
The envelope animation relies heavily on CSS 3D transforms and framer-motion's useScroll.
Audio (music.mp3) is expected to be in the public directory.
Fonts 'Dancing Script' and 'Cormorant Garamond' will be loaded via Google Fonts.
